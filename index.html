About Me

Hello! Welcome to my full stack web development portfolio page! My name is Marley Schneider and I am 22 years old. I recently graduated from Washington State University with a double major in Advertising and Digital Technology and Culture. I am currently working as a Customer Solutions Representative at a company called ChemPoint.

I am passionate about entering a carrer in full stack web development. I am currently enrolled in a 6-month certificate program for full stack developing through the University of Washington (but, Go Cougs)! I am excited to stenghen my developer skills and dive into a career shortly after full of de-bugging and website creativtiy.

When I'm not at work or coding, I enjoy hiking, reading, and spending time with family and friends. I love watching college basketball, and in another life, I would love to be a sports reporter on the sidelines.
